Description of Cleanser

This gentle cleanser removes dirt, excess oil and cleans pores deeply without stripping your skin’s natural moisture.

Benefits

  • Removes impurities and dust
  • Gives a fresh, glowing look
  • Suits all skin types
  • Mild and non-irritating formula
  • Keeps skin soft and hydrated

Key Ingredients

  • Aloe Vera: Soothes and moisturizes skin
  • Green Tea Extract: Fights acne and protects from pollution
  • Vitamin E: Adds glow and reduces dullness
  • Glycerin: Maintains hydration

How to Use

  1. Wet your face with lukewarm water
  2. Take a small amount of cleanser
  3. Massage gently in circular motions for 30 seconds
  4. Rinse well and pat dry
  5. Follow up with serum and moisturizer

Tips for Best Results

  • Use twice daily – morning and night
  • Apply sunscreen after using it in your morning routine
  • Drink plenty of water to enhance skin glow
