Description on sunscreen

Protects your skin from UVA & UVB rays while keeping it moisturized and fresh. This lightweight formula is designed for daily use and suitable for all skin types.

Benefits

  • Non-greasy and quick absorbing
  • Long-lasting broad-spectrum protection
  • SPF 50 shields against harmful sun damage
  • Prevents tanning and dark spots
  • Dermatologically tested

How to Use

  • Apply generously 15 minutes before sun exposure.
  • Reapply every 2 hours for best protection.
  • Use after moisturizer for a smooth finish.

This sunscreen feels feather-light, blends effortlessly, and leaves no white cast. Perfect for daily wear, even under makeup!

Ideal For

  • Prevention of premature aging caused by sun
